Project FrontRooms

Hello!

I'm Cuba, a Spanish multi-disciplinary developer currently leading and working on "Project FrontRooms" — a realistic Roblox Backrooms game that combines RPG-survival, horror, and a non-linear open world, staying fully faithful to the Wikidot and Fandom lore.

This project began as my very first game development experience at 18. While it's often recommended to start with small projects, this one has pushed me to learn far more than I expected — taking me from having no programming knowledge to writing thousands of lines of code, including systems for procedural and infinite world generation.

The idea originally came from watching a Kane Pixels video back in 2022, which got me deeply interested in the Backrooms universe. As a fan of SCP-style concepts, I quickly became immersed in this kind of world. However, at the time, I didn’t see myself building a serious game project.

Instead, I focused on learning the fundamentals: building in Roblox Studio, designing interfaces, programming, and basic animation — gradually improving until reaching where the project stands today.

Active development of the game started in September, when I began working on it more seriously. Since I'm currently handling most of the development on my own, progress can be slow, but steady.

Before diving into the current progress and features, you can check the FAQ section from my Discord server for additional context.
