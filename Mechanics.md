# ⚙️ Mechanics

Our goal is to create **innovative and meaningful gameplay systems** that go far beyond a simple "walking simulator".

Below are the core mechanics currently in development or already implemented.

---

## 🌐 Multiplayer

The game features full multiplayer support. However, due to the nature of certain levels, players may not always be able to directly see each other (e.g., Level 0).

In isolation-based levels, players will instead perceive their friends as a subtle **aura or presence indicator**.

To physically meet another player, both must be located in the same level of the Backrooms.

---

## 📱 iPad & ⌚ Smart Watch

Two core survival devices exist in the game:

- ⌚ The **Smart Watch** is provided at the start of the game  
- 📱 The **iPad** must be purchased at outposts (e.g., Level 1 hubs)

---

# 📱 iPad System

The iPad acts as a central gameplay hub, containing multiple essential applications:

---

## 🧩 Apps Overview

- Berthpedia  
- Profile  
- Inventory  
- Missions  
- Settings  
- Skill Tree  
- Faction Perks  
- Teams  
- Music App  
- Badges  
- Camera  

---

## 📖 Berthpedia

An in-game knowledge database that functions as a **living wiki**.

Entries are unlocked through:
- Photographing entities / objects / levels  
- Surviving encounters  
- Exploring new areas  
- Inspecting or interacting with items  
- Finding or purchasing documents  

---

## 👤 Profile

Displays full player statistics:
- Playtime (hh:mm:ss format)  
- Death count  
- Progression metrics  
- Game completion percentage (based on unlocked badges)  

---

## 🎯 Missions

Faction-based mission system that rewards:
- XP  
- Currency  
- Items  
- Trust points (faction reputation system)  

---

## 🌳 Skill Tree

Two main progression branches:

- 🫀 **Vital Signs:** Health, sanity, stamina, hunger, thirst  
- 🧠 **Survival Skills:** Passive & active abilities  

---

## 🏛️ Faction Perks

Displays benefits depending on faction trust level:
- Discounts  
- Exclusive information  
- Special rewards  

---

## 👥 Teams

Clan system allowing players to:
- Create or join groups  
- Track offline members  
- View stats (level, kills, faction, location)  

---

# ⌚ Watch System

The watch displays:
- Health  
- Sanity  
- Hunger  
- Thirst  

Each is represented as a **quarter-circle UI element**, while stamina is shown in the center.

---

# 🌀 No-Clip System

## 🧠 Concentration System
- Requires focus to activate  
- Skill tree improves success rate  
- Grants % chance of no-clipping  
- Can trigger anywhere  

---

## ⚠️ Null Zones
- Environmental anomalies (floating chairs, particles, etc.)  
- First encounters trigger dialogue: *"reality seems fragile here..."*  

---

## 🧪 Object 92 – ClipSkips

Special anomalous item enabling controlled no-clipping.

---

# 👥 Group No-Clip System

- Shared activation zones pull nearby teammates into same level  
- Proximity system ensures group cohesion  
- Distance separation can trap players in previous levels  

⚠️ Small chance of misrouting:
- The Void  
- No-Clip Fusion  
- No-Clip Derailing  

---

# 🧬 Vital Signs System

Core survival stats:
- Health  
- Hunger  
- Thirst  
- Sanity  
- Stamina  

---

### Gameplay Effects:
- Food has positive and negative effects  
- Almond water is a key survival resource  
- Health regenerates slowly unless critically injured  
- Sanity loss leads to transformation into a **Wretcher**  

---

# 🎭 Qualities

Qualities are **non-P2W randomized traits** obtained with rolls during character creation.

### Examples:

### 🧠 Schizophrenia
- Hallucinations even at high sanity  
- Faster sanity drain  
- Distorted dream states  

### ☣️ Anomalous Allergy
- -20% sanity  
- -5% health when using anomalous objects  

### 👁️ Sixth Sense
- Visualizes sound via icons  
- Requires stationary focus activation  
- Unique perception ability  

### 💬 Charismatic
- +15% trading and shop discounts  

---

# ⚔️ Difficulty & Character Slots

Instead of permanent death, the game uses **character slots** with selectable difficulty.

---

## 🟢 Normal Mode
- Balanced survival  
- Standard entity strength  
- Beginner-friendly  

---

## 🟡 Hard Mode
- Faster stat decay  
- Stronger entities  
- Higher survival pressure  

---

## 🔴 Extreme Mode
- Hard Mode + Permadeath  
- Full restart on death  

---

## 🎮 Character Slots System

- 2 free slots  
- 1 unlockable via game completion  
- 1 premium (GamePass)
